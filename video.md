### Phase 1: Introduction & Tech Stack
*(Action: Start on the landing page, showing the interactive 3D globe.)*

**"Hello everyone! We are thrilled to present Carbonix.** 

**Carbonix is a premium, fully peer-to-peer carbon credit and renewable energy trading platform. Right now, companies looking to offset carbon face massive broker fees and a lack of transparency. We solve that by connecting verified renewable energy producers directly with buyers, fully aligned with India's 2070 Net Zero goals and the UN Paris Agreement.**

**Before we jump into the demo, I want to highlight our rigorous modern tech stack. Our interactive frontend revolves around React utilizing Vite, styled with Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion, and Radix UI. Our robust backend operates on Node.js and Express, paired with MongoDB. We also integrated the Gemini AI API for intelligent market insights, Socket.io for live updates, and Razorpay for seamless, secure transactions."**

### Phase 2: The Consumer (Buyer) Workflow
*(Action: Click 'Get Started' and complete Google Login. Navigate to the marketplace.)*

**"Let's dive into the platform, starting with our first user role: The Consumer or Buyer. When a user securely authenticates via our Google OAuth integration, they are instantly given Consumer access.** 

*(Action: Scroll through the marketplace. Click on a verified solar/wind listing.)*

**As you can see, the buyer has immediate access to our dynamic marketplace.** 
**Input: The buyer can browse through live carbon credit listings, specifically filtering for verified projects backed by standards like Verra or Gold Standard. They input the amount they wish to buy.**

*(Action: Click 'Buy' and show the Razorpay mock payment popup.)*

**Output: Using our fully integrated Razorpay checkout, the payment is processed in real-time. Immediately upon success, the buyer's Consumer Dashboard updates. It perfectly tracks their total capital spent, adds credits to their net energy balance, and generates a downloadable, compliant transaction receipt for their records."**

### Phase 3: The Producer (Seller) Workflow
*(Action: Sign out, then sign back in as an account that has Producer rights. Go to the 'Create Listing' page.)*

**"Now, let's switch to our second role: The Producer, or Seller. Any consumer can request to become a producer to sell their energy.**

*(Action: Type in a quantity of credits, set a price, and select a project type like 'Solar'.)*

**Input: Once approved, a producer can effortlessly create a new listing. Notice how simple the input is: they just enter their certified energy quantity, the project type, and their competitive price point.**

*(Action: Click submit. Navigate to the Seller Dashboard & Market Insights page.)*

**Output: The listing instantly goes live to buyers globally. But more importantly, through our specialized Seller Dashboard, the producer gets granular analytics on their revenue. Furthermore, we leverage Gemini AI to provide dynamic 'Market Insights', analyzing live market trends to give sellers actionable advice on how to price their credits competitively."**

### Phase 4: The Admin Workflow
*(Action: Sign out, and log into your Admin account. Go to the Admin Dashboard and specifically the 'Producer Requests' tab.)*

**"Finally, to maintain absolute platform security and integrity, we have our third role: The Admin.** 

**Input: The Admin portal acts as the global gatekeeper. Here, an admin can review all pending consumer requests to become producers, viewing their company documents and applying approval or rejection inputs.**

*(Action: Click 'Approve' on a pending request. Click through the other tabs: blogs, analytics, users.)*

**Output: By approving a request, the platform instantly upgrades that user’s role in our MongoDB database, granting them selling rights. The Admin also has absolute oversight—tracking global transaction histories, managing eco-blogs, and overseeing all registered users, guaranteeing a fraud-free environment."**
